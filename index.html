How I Became a Freelance Developer : I studied Software Engineering at the university, and Artificial Intelligence was my favorite subject. My graduation thesis was on "A Comparative Study of Machine Learning Algorithms for Predicting Stock Prices". I worked as a talented intern and developer at Fuji Kanko Kaihatsu Co. Ltd, where I developed some travel websites and mobile games. Unfortunately, my father passed away in a car accident, and it was difficult for us to live without him. My mother decided to move to Toronto, where her elder sister lived and ran a small business. After moving to Toronto, I joined her studio, Exceed Robotics, where I learned web and graphic design. However, the salary was not enough to support me and my mother, and her health was not good, so I had to take care of her at home. I decided to look for online jobs, and I started working as a content writer. Writing was my dream, but it was not related to what I had studied so far. I soon realized that I needed some certification, so I started working on freelancing sites. I also studied at Ontario Tech University online and earned a Master's degree in Software Engineering. Working and studying was very tough challenge for me. I considered to give up serveral times. But I had to overcome this obstacle by myself. During those two years, I worked on many freelancing platforms, such as LinkedIn, Upwork, Freelancer, Fiverr, Guru. I learnt many skills like Laravel, React, React Native, Unity, Blockchain technology and became professional in some fields. Being a freelancer developer is very tough way but for a man with solid decision it is a kind of mind controlling course. I like clients with clear requirements and most of them were very generous with good result but keeping timeline and delivering high quality work was very important. I am always ready for new client and make them satisfied with my skills in my professional fields. Thanks for reading..
